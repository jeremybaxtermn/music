Multimedia

Compositions

As part of my role as Music Director at Cornell University's Protestant Cooperative Ministries, I arranged the following suite of short hymn settings for small choir, over the course of 2020-2021. These short pieces of music, performed remotely in these recordings by the PCM Choir, can be used either together or seperately as musical interludes in a worship service, and are perhaps suitable for Lent, although they could be sung for a variety of occasions.

Jeremy Baxter · What Wondrous Love Is This
Jeremy Baxter · Be Thou My Vision

Music Production

This cover of Billie Eilish's "when the party's over" was mixed and mastered for the Cornell University Hangovers a cappella group, in Spring 2020.
