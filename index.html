About Me

Jeremy Baxter is a freelance singer, pianist, director, composer, and audio producer in the Twin Cities area. He graduated with a bachelor of arts in music from Cornell University in 2020, where he directed the Protestant Cooperative Ministries Choir and the a cappella subset of the Cornell University Glee Club.

On this site you will find examples of my work, including music I have composed, mixed, and mastered for various vocal groups. Feel free to contact me using the form linked above, or email me at jeremysbaxter@gmail.com.
